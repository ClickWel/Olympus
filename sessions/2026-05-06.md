4. fprintf prints "You are heading to open the door..."
5. fgets(68 bytes) into stack buffer at rbp-0x30 - overflow by 20 bytes
6. Offset to RIP = 56 bytes (48 byte buffer + 8 byte saved RBP)
7. escape_plan at 0x401255 (prints flag from ./flag.txt)
